I am a Computer Science graduate and a programmer to the my core. Work History


Microsoft

As a Software Development Engineer in Test at Microsoft I focus on creating software to extend automation infrastructure. I am responsible for building and mainting these automation systems, debugging and isolating problems in the code base, and executing creative tests to make sure software is ready for deployment.

Center for Research in Computer Vision

At the CRCV I worked with a PhD student in the development of a novel sparse-representation facial recognition technique for use in videos. This work was accepted for publication in the IEEE Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR) 2013. You can read more about my research here.

University of Central Florida

I worked as a teaching assistant for UCF in the Computer Science I (Algorithms) course and aided in grading of examinations, homework programs, and tutoring students. Additionally I helped teach the Junior Knights program in 2012 which aims to spark students interest in Computer Science in High School.
